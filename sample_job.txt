Senior Site Reliability Engineer
Acme Cloud Inc. — San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)

About Us
Acme Cloud is a fast-growing SaaS platform serving 50M+ users globally. Our infrastructure team ensures 99.99% uptime across a complex microservices architecture running on AWS and Kubernetes.

The Role
We're looking for a Senior SRE to join our Platform Engineering team. You'll own reliability for our core services, drive observability improvements, and build tooling that helps product engineers ship faster and safer.

Responsibilities
- Own availability and performance of production services handling 100K+ requests per second
- Design and implement monitoring, alerting, and observability pipelines using Prometheus, Grafana, and Datadog
- Lead incident response and conduct blameless postmortems to improve system resilience
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, ArgoCD) for 200+ microservices
- Manage Kubernetes clusters (EKS) across multiple AWS regions
- Implement infrastructure-as-code using Terraform and Helm
- Drive SLO/SLI adoption across engineering teams and manage error budgets
- Automate toil reduction — if you're doing it twice, script it
- Participate in on-call rotation and continuously improve runbooks
- Mentor junior SREs and collaborate with product engineering on reliability requirements

Requirements
- 5+ years of experience in SRE, DevOps, or Platform Engineering roles
- Strong proficiency with AWS (EC2, EKS, S3, RDS, Lambda, CloudFront)
- Deep experience with Kubernetes (deployment, networking, troubleshooting, scaling)
- Proficiency in Python or Go for tooling and automation
- Experience with infrastructure-as-code tools (Terraform, Pulumi, or CloudFormation)
- Hands-on experience with observability stacks (Prometheus, Grafana, Datadog, or similar)
- Strong understanding of networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP, load balancing)
- Experience with CI/CD systems (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, ArgoCD, or similar)
- Solid understanding of Linux systems internals
- Excellent incident management and communication skills

Nice to Have
- Experience at high-scale consumer platforms (100M+ users)
- Kubernetes certifications (CKA/CKS)
- AWS certifications (Solutions Architect, DevOps Professional)
- Experience with chaos engineering (Gremlin, Litmus, or custom)
- Contributions to open-source infrastructure tooling
- Experience with cost optimization and FinOps practices

Compensation
$190,000 - $250,000 base + equity + benefits
